After Operation Service (AOS)

AOS - VEHICLE


Location Inspection/Test Specification/Criteria Pass Fail
Items to be Checked
Base Vehicle Platform - Vehicle Exterior(Right side)
Exterior Body (including windows, steps, handles, railings, mounts, bracketry, chains, stepladders, mirrors, boxes etc) Inspect exterior of vehicle for cracks, missing, loose and damaged parts. No crack, missing, loose and damaged parts.
Check for oil leakages. No oil leakage.
Observe for any burning smell. No burning smell.
Cables and Hoses Check cables and hoses for loose and damaged parts. No loose and damaged parts.
Onboard Equipment Check that all are secured. All secured.
Drain Plugs - Right side, Bottom
Drain Plugs Check drain plugs are present. Drain plugs present.
Fire Extinguisher System
Fire Extinguisher 4.5kg Inspect for physical damage. No physical damage.
Check fire extinguisher lever locking pin is disengaged. Lever locking pin is disengaged on fire extinguisher.
Check servicing / hydrostatic expiry date on fire extinguisher. Fire extinguisher servicing / hydrostatic date has not expired.
Pull Cable Assembly Inspect for missing and damaged parts, wear and tear. No missing and damaged parts, wear and tear.
Make sure that lockwire is intact and connected to fire extinguisher. Lockwire not broken and connected to fire extinguisher.
Wheels and Suspension(Right side)
Tyres and CTIS Valves Inspect side wall for cuts and cracks. No cuts and cracks on side wall.
Inspect for tyre wear. (Visual check on wear Indicator) Wear limit should not be lesser than 5mm.
Wheel Rim, Wheel Nuts and Wheel Inspect for oil leakages and damages. No oil leakages and damages.
Inspect for loose and missing wheel nuts. No loose and missing wheel nuts.
Axle (Front and Rear) Inspect for oil leakages and damages from joints and surface. No oil leakages and damages.
Transfer Case Inspect for oil leakages and damages. No oil leakages and damages.
Fuel Tank (Right Side)
Fuel Tank Bin Door Check that locking bin latches are intact and serviceable. Latches are intact and serviceable.
Bins (Right Side)
Bin Doors Check that locking bin latch is intact and serviceable. Latches are intact and serviceable.
Miscellaneous (Right Side)
Auxiliary Power Unit (Signal Variant only) Inspect fuel coupling for signs of fuel leakage at fuel supply and return connection hoses. No leakage.
Inspect interior of APU for signs of oil or fuel leaks. No leakage.
Observe for any burning smell. No burning smell observed.
Fuel Storage Box (Fuel Variant only) Check that locking latch is intact and serviceable. Latches are intact and serviceable.
Logistics Storage Shelf (Logistics variant only) Check that locking latch is intact and serviceable. Latches are intact and serviceable.
Base Vehicle Platform - Vehicle Exterior(Left side)
Exterior Body (including windows, steps, handles, railings, mounts, bracketry, chains, stepladders, mirrors, boxes etc) Inspect exterior of vehicle for cracks, missing, loose and damaged parts. No crack, missing, loose and damaged parts.
Check for oil leakages. No oil leakage.
Observe for any burning smell. No burning smell.
Drain Plugs (Left Side, Bottom)
Drain Plugs Check seal and threaded portion for damages. No damage to seal and threaded portion.
Wheels and Suspension(Left side)
Tyres and CTIS Valves Inspect side wall for cuts and cracks. No cuts and cracks on side wall.
Inspect for tyre wear. (Visual check on wear Indicator) Wear limit should not be lesser than 5mm.
Wheel Rim, Wheel Nuts and Wheel Inspect for oil leakages and damages. No oil leakages and damages.
Inspect for loose and missing wheel nuts. No loose and missing wheel nuts.
Axle (Front and Rear) Inspect for oil leakages and damages from joints and surface. No oil leakages and damages.
Transfer Case Inspect for oil leakages and damages. No oil leakages and damages.
Miscellaneous (Left Side)
Mast Cable Drum (Signal Variant Only) Inspect for physical damage. No physical damage.
Inspect for serviceability of plunger. Plunger serviceable.
Perform functional checks on cable drum. Cable drum rotates smoothly and able to be lowered in a controlled manner.
Inspect RF / GPS cables for cuts and physical damage. No cut and damage.
Check RF / GPS cables for debris and presence of dust cover. No debris and dust cover present.
Miscellaneous (Left Side)
Signal Mast (Signal Variant Only) Check magnetic switch for missing, loose and physical damage. No missing, loose and physical damage.
Check mast for missing, loose and damaged parts. No missing, loose and damaged part.
Inspect pneumatic hoses for cuts. No cut on pneumatic hose.
Logistics Storage Bin (Logistics Variant Only) Inspect exterior of vehicle for cracks, missing, loose and damaged parts. Latches are intact and serviceable.
Base Vehicle Platform - Vehicle Exterior(Front)
Exterior Body (including windows, steps, handles, railings, mounts, bracketry, chains, stepladders, mirrors, boxes etc) Inspect exterior of vehicle for cracks, missing, loose and damaged parts. No crack, missing, loose and damaged parts.
Check for oil leakages. No oil leakage.
Observe for any burning smell. No burning smell.
Ensure serviceability of bonnet latching locks. Locks are able to open and closed.
Cables and Hoses Check cables and hoses for loose and damaged parts. No loose and damaged part.
Vehicle Commander, Driver and Crew Compartment (Interior Cabin)
Onboard Equipment Check that all equipment are secured. All secured.
Vetronics (Interior Cabin)
Docking Station Check that VC / TC docking station mount is able to lock, unlock and secure C2 laptop. VC / TC docking station mount able to lock, unlock and secure C2 laptop.
Weapon Systems
Remote Machine Gun (RMG) (Except Medical Variant) Check that limiter is present and not damaged. Limiter is present and not damaged.
Smoke Grenade Launcher (SGL) (Security, Recce, Engineer and Signal Variants Only) Check for unused smoke grenade in launcher tubes. No unused smoke grenade in launcher tube.
Miscellaneous (Roof)
Signal Mast (Signal Variant Only) Check that canvas cover is placed over top of signal mast and secured. Top of signal mast covered and canvas cover secured.
Operation of the Vehicle
Switch Off Engine Check underneath for any damage, loose components and signs of fluid leaks. No damage, loose component and sign of fluid leakage.
